About Me

Mary Khoury

I’m a passionate artist and designer who grew up in Damascus, Syria. I graduated with an Illustration BFA from Syracuse University, and I love creating art that can tell stories and designs that can inspire others.

I work in a variety of styles to match each project. I enjoy working with bright colors as well as cute or realistic aesthetics, and I like creating fantasies and myths that still reflect the real world in their own way.
