Illustrator & Designer

Mary Khoury

I’m a passionate and aspiring artist and designer from Damascus, Syria. When my Syrian credentials weren’t accepted, I decided to pursue my passion for the arts and graduated with a multimedia design associate degree from OCC and a bachelor’s degree in illustration from Syracuse University. My main focus is to create art that can tell stories and designs that can inspire others.
I work in a variety of styles to adapt and match the projects I’m working on. I mainly work digitally, but I also enjoy working with traditional mediums and utilizing them in my digital works. I particularly enjoy working with vibrant colors as well as cute or realistic aesthetics (or a mix of both!) and when it comes to illustration, I like to draw fantasies and myths, and deliver stories that reflect the real world even if they look nothing like it.
